INT. COFFEE SHOP - DAY

Rain streaks the window. MAYA sits alone at a corner table, stirring a cold cup of coffee she hasn't touched. Her phone lies face-down beside her, screen dark.

INT. COFFEE SHOP - CONTINUOUS

The phone buzzes once. Maya stares at it without moving, jaw tight, hand hovering above the table.

EXT. COFFEE SHOP - CONTINUOUS

She pushes through the door into the rain, phone pressed to her ear, umbrella forgotten inside. Cars blur past on the wet street behind her.

EXT. CITY STREET - CONTINUOUS

Maya stops walking. She lowers the phone slowly, rain soaking her shoulders, her expression breaking open somewhere between relief and disbelief.

EXT. CITY STREET - CONTINUOUS

She looks up at the grey sky, exhales, and starts laughing through the rain, alone on the empty sidewalk.

EXT. CITY STREET - NIGHT

Streetlights flicker on. Maya walks home slowly, hands in her pockets, the city glowing gold and blurred around her in the wet dark.
